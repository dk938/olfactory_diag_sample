診断結果

あなたのタイプは:

Balanced type

Always calm and rational, with a sense of humor.

The fundamental trends

You have the talent to take an appropriate distance from your emotions and tend to always think calmly. Even under pressure, your thinking remains little affected and you know your limits. Contrasting with the apparent calm of your appearance, your sense of humor is also attractive.

On the other hand, you may sometimes have trouble understanding the emotions of others. Some may be aware of their low emotional expressiveness. As a result, you may tend to avoid social interactions and feel anxiety in communication. In addition, you may find it difficult to work in a team, which can make cooperating with others complicated. Sometimes you could give a cold impression to others and feel uncomfortable expressing affection.

You tend to think about things from a unique point of view, privileging reason over emotions, and trying to grasp things from an original angle.

Recommended flavors

Geranium

The scent of geranium has a relaxing and balancing effect on emotions. This effect is due to components such as citronellol and geraniol. Balanced people are enthusiastic about new information and knowledge, but tend to lose their emotional balance when plans or expectations are not realized. The scent of geranium can help restore this balance.
